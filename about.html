The Heart Behind Meycake

Meycake was created by two friends Isha and Mai who share a love for baking and a passion for bringing joy through beautifully crafted desserts. What began as a small idea soon became a dream project built with creativity, dedication, and countless hours in the kitchen. Today, Meycake continues to grow with the same heart and purpose, to bake cakes that feel warm, personal, and made with love. Every slice reflects our commitment to quality from premium ingredients to thoughtful designs that make every celebration special..

Today, we continue crafting each cake with premium ingredients, artistic detail, and a warm heart. Every slice tells a story sweet, memorable, and made with love.
